About Me

Hi, it's Mee. I’m a video editor with 3 years of experience working across multiple brands and content formats. My work spans documentary-style editing, social media and reel edits, and branded content. Beyond editing, I actively direct shoots and contribute as a content strategist, helping shape ideas from concept to final delivery.

I've also written scripts for reel ads, made a plugin for After Effects, developed an image upscaler/enhancer, and designed an Instagram filter.

Multimedia and Tech is where I thrive - connecting visuals, sound, motion, and narrative.
